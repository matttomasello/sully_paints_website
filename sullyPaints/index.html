Face Painting

Boston professional face painter providing local and remote fun for an array of events including Festivals, Weddings, Birthday, Fundraisers, Corporate Parties, and more

Hi, my name is McKenzie and I have over a decade of experience working in creative makeup that has branched to weddings and editorials with names like Vogue, New Balance, and many more. Face painting has become my newest passion and I focus and practice as much as my previous accomplishments to hone in on it's creative potential. I love evoking a feeling of fantasy and creativity in all ages and backgrounds. I value maintaining professionalism while enjoying a bright energy at every event.
